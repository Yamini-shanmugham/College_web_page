Hostel

Hostel facilities are embarked in such a way, that the students can feel homely and pursue their studies in a serene atmosphere. Our institution provides separate hostel facilities for girls with 64 rooms and has all necessary facilities for boarding and other recreational activities, to support goodwill, sociability and to play essential role in promoting community living among them.

The canteen is located inside the College. The hostellers and day scholars are provided meals by assuring food safety and quality. The students who come from faraway places, start their journey to the College early in the morning. The mid-day meal is provided. Even many day-scholars use canteen facilities for their breakfast and evening tea with snacks.They are provided with three meals a day, bed coffee and evening tea/coffee. The Management of the Canteen is directly under the control of the College Administration.

Indoor Stadium

The Stadium is a suitable place to organize National as well as International level indoor sports events, such as Volleyball, Table-Tennis, Gymnastics, Handball, Boxing, Wrestling, Basketball, Badminton etc. and can be hired for such events. It possesses 63 mtr x 33 mtr size Sports Arena with necessary players' rooms, commentators' rooms, snack bars and other essential facilities. It is also an ideal venue for various other programs like music concerts, drama, fashion shows, seminars, conferences, exhibitions, cultural programs and so on and can be hired for the same.
